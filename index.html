Built with purpose.

Rogers Holdings is family-owned and built around steady work, practical judgment, and responsibility to the people connected to each business.

We believe strong businesses are shaped by dependable service, clear values, and the willingness to keep improving the details that customers and communities feel every day.

As Rogers Holdings grows, the goal is to build a portfolio that reflects those standards instead of chasing growth for its own sake.
